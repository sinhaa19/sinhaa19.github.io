Tour Of Bhutan

Bhutan, a country that maps its progress via GNH (Gross National Happiness), rightfully features as a top riding destination. The imagination of a rider is fed all through, cruising at an average of 10 curves per km of road, jaw-dropping landscapes and cities that don’t even have traffic lights. This ride will catapult itself into yet another unforgettable experience.
Origin Siliguri
Distance 1259 kms
Terrain Tarmac, Twisties, Dirt
Duration 12 days
Price Rider - INR 38000, Couple - INR 76000.

Destination: Bhutan

Coastal Trail

Surf’s Up! Ride through the lesser known back roads that line the west coast of India. Catch the best view of the waves and get up, close and personal with the cuisine and culture of the 5 states that line this jewelled coastline. Royal Enfield Coastal Trail is a 12-days ride that goes through Maharashtra, Goa, Karnataka, Kerala and Tamil Nadu, running close to the seafront, passing through some of the most pristine virgin beaches and beautiful landscapes. Riders are invited to sign up to experience this one-of-a-kind coastal adventure.
Distance 1765 Kms
Terrain Coastal backroads
Duration 12 Days
Price INR 22,000/- for single registration & INR 44,000/- for couple registration

After Dark Ride

A ride like never before, to experience the adventure of riding under the starlit open skies amidst the vast Rajasthan landscape and palatial havelis. After Dark had 12 riders on Royal Enfield motorcycles ride out from Jodhpur and riding through some of the most scenic locations before culminating at Bikaner. Riding at Night opens up a completely new dimension introducing one to sights and sounds around us which we miss out in the daytime. This ride also brought out the different aesthetic appeal of the Royal Enfield motorcycle at this hour.
Origin Luni (Jodhpur)
Distance 900 kms
Terrain Tarmac, Sand Dunes, Flatlands
Duration 6 days
Destination Gajaner (Bikaner).

Date: December 4-9, 2018
