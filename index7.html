On-Site Repair

For all minor faults and an immediate repair thereof, a technician will be despatched to the breakdown location to attend to the motorcycle. While Royal Enfield shall bear labour and conveyance, extra Parts & materials will be charged on actuals.

Vehical Accident

In case of accidents or when the motorcycle can’t be repaired on the spot, Royal Enfield shall assist in transporting the motorcycle to the nearest Royal Enfield service center for repairs.

Battery Drain

In case of a weak or dead battery, a technician shall be despatched who will attempt to jumpstart the motorcycle. In case a battery change is required, the technician shall fix a new battery. The cost of the battery shall be charged on actuals. In case the problem is not fixed, Royal Enfield shall also assist in transporting the motorcycle to the nearest Royal Enfield service center..

Vehical Breakdown

In case of the motorcycle facing major faults that can’t be fixed at the location, the motorcycle will be transported, free of cost, to the nearest Royal Enfield Authorised service center for repairs..

Flat Tyre Assisstance

Incase of a flat tyre, a Royal Enfield technician will be despatched to your location to attend to the motorcycle. Cost of fixing the flat will be charged on actuals.

Key Lockout

Incase you have broken or misplaced your Key, a brand new set of keys will be couriered to the address of your choice..

Medical Referral

In case you require medical assistance while on a ride, Royal Enfield will refer you to the nearest medical center. Cost of treatment/consultation shall need to be paid on actuals to the Medical centre
